for loops
==================================

for item in iterrable_object:
	do something
	
Iterable objects are lists, str, ranges


ranges
---------------------------------------
range is used to genereate immutable sequence of numbers


range(7) : gives integers from 0 to 6
range(1,8) : gives integers from 1 to 7
range(1,10,2) : gives 1,3,5,7,9
range(7,0,-1) gives 7,6,5,4,3,2,1



while loops
========================================

while <boolean expression>:
	do something
